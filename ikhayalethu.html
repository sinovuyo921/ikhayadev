Description

Ikhaya Lethu - Our Home - Development is a Non-profit Organisation (NPO) that is dedicated on serving the youth with support and help regarding Career Awareness and Online Applications to Universities and Busaries. We also provide a plartform for practicing and expressing Artistic Activities such as Drama and Poetry. Featured activities include the Book Club and Dance.



VISION

Our vision is to increase qualified leaners to tertiary institution, number of Artist in rural areas, to fight the high level of drop in schools, to reduce Drug and alcohol abuse and to improve the level of knowledge in terms Emails address and online application in high schools.




MISSION

Our intentions are to bring development, Entertainment, Better Standard of living, better education and better future for the youth of our country.
